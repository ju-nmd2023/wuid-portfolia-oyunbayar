Coffee Mug Mockup

This is my last project of Fundementals of Grapic Design course. Designing a logo for a newly opened coffee shop in Jönköping is a captivating project that involves merging the essence of the city with the identity of the coffee shop. To begin, thorough research of Jönköping's culture and the coffee shop's theme is essential. Consider incorporating local symbols or characteristics into the design to make it unique.
The conceptualization phase involves brainstorming ideas that convey the desired emotions and messages. Sketch rough ideas on paper, exploring various elements like coffee cups, beans, and steam. Transition to digital drafts, experimenting with fonts, colors, and layouts. Ensure the logo is scalable and visually appealing in different sizes.
